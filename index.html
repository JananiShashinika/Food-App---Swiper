About Us

Welcome to Cafe Ceylon !

Discover Cafe Ceylon,where modern dining meets Sri Lankan charm in the heart of Colombo 7.
We are not just a cafe; we are a celebration of modern dining with a touch of Sri Lankan charm.
At Cafe Ceylon, we pride ourselves on serving a delightful fusion of international flavors and beloved Ceylon classics, creating a unique dining experience that tantalizes your taste buds and warms your heart. Our menu is a carefully curated collection of modern culinary delights, featuring mouthwatering burgers, hearty sandwiches, and aromatic rice dishes that are sure to satisfy any craving. Each dish is crafted with love and the freshest ingredients, promising a burst of flavor in every bite. But Cafe Ceylon is more than just about great food. It's about a place where friends gather, families bond, and everyone feels at home. Our welcoming atmosphere, combined with a passion for hospitality, makes us the perfect spot for catching up over a cup of our signature Ceylon tea or enjoying a leisurely meal. Located in the picturesque and bustling Colombo 7, Cafe Ceylon offers an inviting ambiance that seamlessly blends tradition and modernity. Whether you're here to grab a quick bite or to indulge in a relaxing afternoon, we invite you to experience the warmth and comfort of our cafe. Join us at Cafe Ceylon and embark on a culinary journey where tradition meets innovation. Let our flavors tell you the story of a modern cafe with a Ceylonese heart.
